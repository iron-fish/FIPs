## Abstract
This FIP specifies the dates and changes for the first Iron Fish Hardfork.
## Specification
- Codename: Hardfork 1
- Activation:
    - Block >= 246_468 on the Iron Fish [Testnet](https://testnet.explorer.ironfish.network/), estimated October 18 2023
    - Block >= 302_464 on the Iron Fish [Mainnet](https://explorer.ironfish.network/), estimated November 15 2023

- Included FIPs:
    - [FIP-1](https://fips.ironfish.network/fips/fip-allow-asset-owners-to-change-owner-key) Allow asset owners to change owner key
    - [FIP-2](https://fips.ironfish.network/fips/fip-2) Enforce Sequential Block Time
